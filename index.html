Our Journey to Success

The Asia Cargo Services was inaugurated in 2000 as a sole proprietor with five major destinations and serving delivery restricted to Lahore, Multan, Faisalabad, Rawalpindi & Peshawar. As industry developed in Pakistan, and more raw materials and other products were to be dispatched nationwide, the need for a cargo service grew, and The Asia Cargo found itself fulfilling a bigger need-helping local industry grow. As a result of this business growth and more facilitate customers, The Asia Cargo Service decided to register with partnership in 2002.
